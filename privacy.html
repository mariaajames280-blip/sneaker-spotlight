Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Sneaker-Spotlight. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle information when you visit our sneaker review and comparison website.

2. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information to operate our website effectively:

Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent, referring sites (via analytics)
  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, IP address

Information You Provide

  • Contact Forms: Name, email address, message content
  • Comments: If enabled, information you post in comments

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To analyze website traffic and improve our content
  • To respond to your inquiries through our contact form
  • To monitor and prevent technical issues or abuse

4. Affiliate Links & Third Parties

Sneaker-Spotlight uses affiliate marketing links, primarily to Amazon. When you click these links:

  • Amazon places cookies on your device to track the referral
  • We may earn a commission if you make a qualifying purchase
  • We have no access to Amazon's tracking data or your purchase information

We also use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our site. You can opt-out of Google Analytics tracking.

5. Your Rights & Choices

  • You can disable cookies in your browser settings
  • You can opt-out of Google Analytics tracking
  • You can contact us to request deletion of personal information you've provided

6. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our contact form.
